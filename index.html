Your Questions Answered

Do you offer personal training? +

Yes, we offer certified one-on-one personal coaching packages. You can choose from single sessions or discounted bulk packages. Enquire at the front desk or via our WhatsApp chat for details!

Is there a free trial? +

Absolutely! We offer a one-day free trial so you can experience our facility, equipment, and atmosphere before committing. Use the 'Book Free Trial' form above to register.

Do you have women-only timings? +

While we are a co-ed facility, we offer dedicated women-only group fitness classes every weekday evening for those who prefer them. The gym floor is open to all during all operating hours.

What facilities are included? +

Your membership includes access to all strength, cardio, and recovery zones, locker rooms, showers, and most group classes. Personal training and nutrition plans are available as add-ons.
